When 2.3 Million Patients Can't Reach Their Pharmacy

Healthcare networks fail compliance, patients suffer, and millions in penalties loom. Traditional analysis takes weeks and misses critical gaps.

I built an intelligent geospatial system that revolutionized how Kroger Health ensures every patient can access life-saving medications when they need them most.

The Challenge

Healthcare regulators mandate that 95% of patients must reach a pharmacy within specific time and distance thresholds. Miss this target? Face millions in penalties and potentially lose network certification.

The existing process was a nightmare: manual calculations, outdated assumptions, and weeks of analysis that still missed critical coverage gaps in rural and underserved areas.

With regulatory audits approaching and expansion decisions pending, leadership needed real-time, precise, actionable intelligence.

My Solution

I designed an end-to-end geospatial analytics pipeline that processes millions of patient records and calculates real-world accessibility with surgical precision.

By integrating live routing APIs, advanced spatial algorithms, and cloud data infrastructure, I created a system that delivers insights in hours, not weeks.

The result? 100% regulatory compliance, proactive gap identification, and data-driven expansion strategies that directly impact patient care quality.

Technical Architecture & Innovation

🏗️ Scalable Data Pipeline

Engineered high-performance ETL processes in Snowflake handling 2M+ de-identified patient records with optimized spatial indexing and partitioning strategies for sub-second query responses.

🧮 Advanced Spatial Analytics

Implemented sophisticated geospatial algorithms using Python GeoPandas and Shapely, including coordinate transformations, buffer analysis, and custom polygon intersection calculations for precise coverage mapping.

🚗 Real-World Route Intelligence

Integrated Google Maps and OpenRouteService APIs with intelligent fallback mechanisms, processing 500K+ route calculations while accounting for traffic patterns, road conditions, and accessibility constraints.

📊 Executive Dashboards

Built dynamic Power BI visualizations with drill-down capabilities, interactive heat maps, and automated alerting systems that enable C-suite and operations teams to make instant, informed decisions.

⚡ Performance Optimization

Achieved 95% performance improvement through parallel processing, intelligent caching strategies, and custom spatial indexing that reduced analysis time from 3 weeks to 4 hours.

🔄 Automated Compliance Monitoring

Deployed continuous monitoring workflows with automated report generation, regulatory threshold tracking, and proactive alerting for coverage gaps before they become compliance issues.
